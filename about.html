Welcome to Hotel Surya Restaurant and Lodge

Welcome to Hotel Surya Restaurant and Lodge, your peaceful holiday getaway nestled in the heart of Nepal’s vibrant landscape. We offer a warm atmosphere, comfortable accommodations, and delectable local cuisine - all at a price that won't break the bank.

We offer a unique blend of traditional hospitality and modern comforts, ensuring a memorable stay for all our guests.

At Hotel Surya, we give you a home-like feeling with cozy rooms and tasty local food. Join us at Hotel Surya Restaurant and Lodge for a special food experience and warm welcome. Every meal here is something to celebrate. Your stay will be a relaxing break from the outside world.

Core Value

At Hotel Surya, we offer more than just a room - we offer a welcoming family atmosphere. Our friendly staff embodies Nepal's genuine hospitality, taking care of every detail to ensure a flawless and culturally-authentic experience. From delicious local cuisine to traditional touches, we'll help you feel right at home and immersed in the rich heritage of Nepal.

Mission

Hotel Surya's mission is to be a sanctuary of unparalleled Nepali hospitality. We aim to delight guests with comfortable stays, mouthwatering cuisine, and enriching cultural experiences - all while being responsible stewards of our environment and communities.

Vision

Our vision is to elevate the way the world experiences Nepal's treasures by pioneering sustainable tourism practices. We aspire to set the hospitality benchmark through innovative services that preserve authenticity while adopting modern conveniences seamlessly.
